Reading Journal Project

A reading record web app and a personal marketing case study. I use this project to practice positioning, UX improvements, and content strategy for learners.

Goal

My goal is to learn digital marketing through a real product. I plan, improve the experience, communicate the value, and document what I learn.

Target users

  • People who enjoy reading (especially Japanese manga)
  • Language learners who want to keep Japanese and English titles/notes
  • Anyone who prefers a calm, simple journaling experience

Positioning & value

  • Separate Japanese and English titles/notes to reduce confusion
  • Simple, calm UI inspired by a reading journal
  • Designed for quick logging and easy reviewing
